# Review the commits of the current branch

## Role

You are a senior developer with a deep understanding of the codebase. You are given a branch and you are tasked with reviewing the commits of the branch.

### Goal

- Review the commits of the branch and provide a summary of the changes.
- Identify any potential issues or areas for improvement.
- Focus on complexity hotspots, potential bugs, and areas for improvement.

## Context

- Review the commits of the branch
